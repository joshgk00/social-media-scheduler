## Decisions Made

- Mocked `associateMediaToPost` at module level via `vi.mock` rather than letting it fall through to DB-level mock chains. This is cleaner, avoids the nested-transaction mock complexity (associateMediaToPost internally calls `db.transaction()`), and makes assertions direct and readable.
- Passed `tx` (the transaction handle from the outer `db.transaction` callback) to `associateMediaToPost`. In Drizzle with postgres-js, passing a transaction handle to `.transaction()` reuses the outer transaction as a savepoint — correct and atomic behavior.
- Used `undefined`-vs-empty-array sentinel: `undefined` means "don't touch media", `[]` means "clear all associations". This mirrors the existing `tagIds` pattern for consistency.

## Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation | Status |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|------------|--------|
| T-6.4-01 | Spoofing | post.service.ts mediaIds | accept | Single-user app — no cross-user media ownership to spoof. If multi-user is added, add ownership check in `associateMediaToPost`. | closed |
| T-6.4-02 | Tampering | post.service.ts mediaIds | mitigate | `mediaIds` validated as `z.array(z.string().uuid()).optional()` at `shared/schemas/posts.ts:13`. `associateMediaToPost` called inside `db.transaction()` in `post.service.ts`. `WHERE postId IS NULL` guard in `media.service.ts:292` prevents hijacking already-assigned media. | closed |
| T-6.4-03 | Denial of Service | post.service.ts mediaIds | mitigate | Body size limit: `express.json({ limit: '1mb' })` at `app.ts:57`. Bounded UPDATE loop in `media.service.ts:288-293` — one UPDATE per admitted UUID, capped by the 1MB body limit. | closed |
| T-6.4-04 | Information Disclosure | post.service.ts mediaIds | accept | Invalid/non-existent `mediaIds` are silently skipped — no rows updated, no error leaks existence of other media IDs. | closed |
| T-6.4-05 | Tampering | routes/posts.ts mediaIds | mitigate | POST route validates via `createPostSchema.safeParse()` at `routes/posts.ts:98-101`; PATCH via `updatePostSchema.safeParse()` at `routes/posts.ts:229-232`. Both reject non-UUID `mediaIds` with 400 before service is called. | closed |

*Status: open · closed*

| Color | Hex | Usage |

## Color Usage Guidelines

- **Dark red (`#640f0d`)** is the dominant brand color. It appears in the logo, pricing badges, geometric background textures, and section headers. Use it to convey brand identity.
- **Accent red (`#ed474a`)** is brighter and used sparingly for interactive elements -- buttons, links, hover states. It draws attention to calls to action.
- **Charcoal (`#231f20`)** is the primary text and logo color. Pair with white for high contrast.
- Backgrounds alternate between white and light gray to create visual separation between sections.
- Dark photography overlays (dark charcoal at ~70% opacity) are used for parallax/hero sections with white text on top.

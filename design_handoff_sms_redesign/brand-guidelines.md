# Clicks & Mortar Websites - Brand Guidelines

## Company Name

- **Full name**: Clicks & Mortar Websites
- **Abbreviation**: CMW (internal use only)
- **Tagline**: "Building websites without the upfront costs"
- Use the ampersand (&), not "and" in the company name

## Mission

To give small businesses and nonprofits access to professional, credible websites at a low monthly cost with no down payment -- because the traditional pricing model doesn't work for businesses that don't have thousands of dollars in upfront capital.

## Core Value Propositions

1. **No upfront costs** - No down payment, unlike competitors who charge 50%+ upfront
2. **Cash flow friendly** - Simple monthly payments that fit a small business budget
3. **Worry free** - 30 minutes of monthly content updates included
4. **Always current** - Free website redesign/refresh every 3 years
5. **Mobile friendly** - Responsive design across all devices
6. **Focus on your business** - CMW handles the website so the owner can run their business

## Competitive Positioning

| CMW | Traditional Web Firms |
|-----|----------------------|
| No down payment | 50% down ($2,000+) |
| Monthly payments | Lump sum or installments |
| Hosting included | Hosting separate |
| Monthly updates included | Updates billed hourly |
| Free redesign every 3 years | Pay again for redesign |

## Standard Pricing (Subscription Model)

| Plan | Monthly Cost | Includes |
|------|-------------|----------|
| **Website Only** | $99.95/month | Unlimited pages, 1 add-on, hosting, 30 min updates/month, 3-year refresh |
| **Complete Start-Up Package** | $249.95/month | Everything above + copywriting (7 pages), logo design, business cards, brochure, social media covers, blog posts (2/month), SEO submissions, Google Places, citations, directory submissions, reporting |

## Custom Project Pricing

For redesigns or custom-scoped work outside the subscription model:
- Total project cost quoted upfront
- Payment broken over a term (typically 24 months)
- Hosting billed separately at $20/month
- Proposal generated for client approval before work begins

## Visual Identity

- See `brand-colors.md` for full color palette

- Logo: House roofline over "Clicks & MORTAR Websites" with brick texture on "MORTAR"
- Photography style: Professional but warm; dark overlays on hero images
- Geometric red backgrounds used for accent sections (low-poly/faceted style)
